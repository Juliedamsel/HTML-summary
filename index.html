ASPILOS FOUNDATION

Aspilos Foundation is a youth led non-profit using technology and digital inclusion to drive economic growth and good governance. The mission is to use technology to:

  • Provde innovative and sustainable pathway to economic empowerment for young people.
  • Increase citizen participation in governance so they can demand accountability and good governance from public office holders.

The foundation has its organisation spread across various cities in Nigeria such as:

  1. Abuja
  2. Kaduna State
  3. Niger State
  4. Ogun State.

Aspilos Foundation offer various courses ranging from; Web development, data sciences, cyber security etc.
Aspiloa Foundation abuja branch is currently is session as they commenced on the 13th day of July 2023 with a total number of 6 students participating in the physical class and a total of 30 students from different parts of Nigeria participating in the Virtual class.
The physical class which has a total of six students was grouped into:

  1. Web developmnt class cordinated by Miss Odunze Jennifer
  2. Data Science class cordinated by Mrs Grace Okeke

These classes were grouped, depending on the students point of interest with each class comprising of 3 students in each class.

The foundation is also geared towards ensuring that the benefactors of this noble organization not only learn but also ensure that these young ladies are projected to the society and contribute back to the society by sponsoring some of their brilliant innovations. To achieve this, the organization also established a forum called The start and grow forum in order to groom their ideas and innovations and as well as get external NGO's and body to sponsor these innovations.
